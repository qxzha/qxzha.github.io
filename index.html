About Me

I am currently a Second-year master student in Department of Computer Science and Teconology at Huaqiao University and a member of Media Analysis and Data Mining Group led by Prof. Yang Gao, fortunately supervised by Prof. Xin Liu. My research interest lies in noisy correspondence learning, multimodal learning, multimodal retrieval-augmented generation and large multimodal models.
